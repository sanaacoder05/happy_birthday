In the Story of Our Lives

In the story of our lives, there was a friend so bright,
Their smile, like stars, lit up our darkest night.
They were kind and caring, like the ocean wide,
With a heart full of love, they stood by our side.

When we felt low, they lifted our spirits high,
Their laughter was a song that reached the sky.
Through thick and thin, they never left our side,
A friend like them, in them, we confide.

Though time moves on, their memory stays strong,
In our hearts forever, where they belong.
For a friend so special, so rare and true,
We're grateful for you, and all that you do.
